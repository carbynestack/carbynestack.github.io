Collaborative computing on encrypted data.

Carbyne Stack relies on Secure Multiparty Computation (MPC) to keep data encrypted end-to-end: in transit, at rest, and in use. By implementing a client-server MPC model, Carbyne Stack allows any number of clients to collaborate securely on private data. No sharing with a trusted third party is necessary.

Federated to serve.

Carbyne Stack federates cloud instances into multi-party virtual clouds that provide distributed decentralized services for storing and processing secret-shared data. Clients can consume these services to securely offload computations on sensitive data.

MPC born cloud native.

Carbyne Stack rests on the shoulders of giants to lift Secure Multiparty Computation into the cloud: Containerization and orchestration with Kubernetes, Istio for flexible routing, and Knative to deploy MPC as serverless workloads. Carbyne Stack can be deployed to any Kubernetes cluster, on premise and in the cloud.
